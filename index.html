About Map

Availability of healthcare in a geographic area is very essential to mankind. Good and quality healthcare give a good measure on standard of leaving and impact on mortality rate. How accessible a healthcare facility is in a vicinity is becoming very crucial with recent outbreak of pandemic. Access to health care could be determined with geographic distance as well as travel time.

A constant buffer radius is created around a hospital to identify a catchment area when the healthcare facility is clicked. This area depicts the people in the vicinity who could have access to the healthcare facility via walking.
