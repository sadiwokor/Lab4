Accessibility

Availability of healthcare in a geographic area is very essential to mankind. Good and quality healthcare give a good measure on standard of leaving and impact on mortality rate. How accessible a healthcare facility is in a vicinity is becoming very crucial with recent outbreak of pandemic. Access to health care could be determined with geographic distance as well as travel time.

The use of BUFFER as a proximity analysis tool was adopted. A constant buffer radius is created around a hospital to identify a catchment area when the healthcare facility is clicked. This area depicts the people in the vicinity who could have access to the healthcare facility via walking.

Occupancy Status

As population increase in a geographic space, demand on housing increase. There is therefore the need to make future projections on the demand and supply of housing. In this regard, an extrapolated estimate on OCCUPANCY STATUS was generated based solely on sampling. This estimate is derived from the US Census America Community Survey (ACS) data which are continually collected. This data was from King County website and provides a multi-year statistical estimate for selected demographic, social, economic and housing characteristics.

Clicking or hovering over the estimated occupancy housing status Polygon will display a chart between Estimated Occupied housing Unit and Estimated Vacancy unit. The plot helps the government to make future predictions of areas with King county to have low vacancy in housing and areas that could have relatively high occupancy status.
